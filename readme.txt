== Description ==

Up Posts is a widget in you sidebar that allows you to display your upcoming posts to your readers. This will create anticipation. 
More importantly, the widget gets your reader to come back to your blog.

Here are some options:

*  Number of upcoming posts to display
*  Choose whether to show scheduled posts, drafts, or both.
*  Include link to sign-up to newsletter

== Installation ==

1. Upload `Up Posts Widget.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to Appearance > Widgets to place the widget in the sidebar.
3. Press Save and go to your site to see it in action.
